Membership and Policies

Become a Member

Become a member to get free access to all our resources!

Requirements:
  1. Proof of address
  2. University card and ID / Personal card / Driving license

Key Policies

Borrowing Periods

4 weeks maximum borrowing period

Fines

Overdue fines are $0.5/day per book

Lost/Damaged Items

Patrons are responsible for replacement cost

Additional Policies:

Books can be renewed up to 2 times if there are no holds on them. Renewals can be done online or in person.

Members can reserve books that are currently checked out. You will be notified when the book becomes available.

Please maintain quiet study environment. Food and drinks are allowed only in designated areas.
